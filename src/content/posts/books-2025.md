In this blog I would like to share the books I have read in 2025, and with a short review for each

How good a books is measured by how it changes my thinking or behavior.

## Books Read in 2025

### Elon Musk

Start from principles. Take risk. And checklist is very useful.

### Trading and Exchanges

A must read if you want to understand the financial market. It explains the bid-ask spread, market making, arbitrage, and many other concepts in detail.

### Poor Charlie's Almanack  

A must read of anyone no matter who you are.

Listened to the audiobook version of this book during my road trip from NY state to Seattle.

One key takeaway from this book is the importance of multidisciplinary learning.

The world is complex, so no single discipline can provide 

### The Selfish Gene

Also listened to the audiobook in my NY to Seattle road trip.

The foundational idea of this books teaches me is that genes also defines the behavior of living beings, including humans.

### How to Read a Book

Always read the content page first.

### Make it Stick

Recall is the best way to consolidate learning.

### The Philosophy of Software Design

A must read of software engineers.

It explains the key principles of software design - manage complexity, information hiding, and modularity.

### Head First Design Patterns

Has relationship is better than inheritance in many cases.

### A Tour of C++

Only went through the the first 1/4 of the book. But like the style of the book. If you want to learn C++, this is a good start. It explains instead of just listing out the features of C++.

### Code Complete

A must read for software engineers.

Include same ideas covered in the philosophy of software design, and provides more aspects of the full life cycle of software development, e.g. requirements gathering, testing, debugging, etc.
